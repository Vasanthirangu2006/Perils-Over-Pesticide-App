#  Agri Saathi – Smart Farming Assistant

##  Overview
Agri Saathi is a smart agriculture support platform designed to assist farmers in identifying crop issues and adopting sustainable farming practices. It provides an intuitive interface for crop guidance and pest-related insights.

---

##  Problem Statement
Farmers often face challenges in identifying crop diseases and pests at early stages, leading to reduced yield and excessive pesticide usage.Lack of accessible digital tools worsens the issue. Inadequate awareness among farmers regarding the correct dosage of pesticides for different crops and acreage results in excessive chemical usage, reduced productivity, and long-term environmental damage.

---

##  Solution
Agri Saathi provides a user-friendly platform that helps farmers:
- Identify crop-related issues  
- Get guidance on pest control  
- Promote sustainable farming practices  

---

##  Features
-  Crop issue identification (prototype)  
-  Pest detection interface (UI-based)  
-  Simple and intuitive dashboard  
-  User-friendly design for accessibility  
-  Focus on sustainable agriculture
